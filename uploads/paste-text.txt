EXTENSION: FACULTY DASHBOARD (INSTRUCTOR PAGE)

This section expands the platform to include the instructor experience.

The system enables a "Faculty Tracking Mode" where students can optionally allow their instructor to monitor their performance and receive AI-generated reports.

Goal:
Enhance clinical training by combining AI evaluation with human supervision.

---

FEATURE: STUDENT CONSENT (TRACKING ACTIVATION)

Inside Student Page:

Prompt:
"Do you allow your instructor to track your performance and help you improve your clinical skills?"

Options:

* Allow Tracking ✅
* Decline ❌

IF Allow:

* Activate Faculty Tracking Mode
* Link student performance to instructor dashboard
* Sync data with Blackboard Ultra

---

PAGE NAME:
Faculty Clinical Performance Dashboard

---

SECTION 1: OVERVIEW PANEL

Display:

* List of students

* Status indicators:
  🟢 Good Performance
  🟡 Needs Improvement
  🔴 Critical Weakness

* Number of attempts per student

* Completion rate

---

SECTION 2: REAL-TIME ALERTS (PROACTIVE NOTIFICATIONS)

System sends alerts to instructor when:

* Student repeats same mistake
* Critical step skipped (e.g., Hand Hygiene)
* Performance below threshold

Notification Example:

"Alert: Student Ahmed has completed the venipuncture simulation 5 times.
Repeated weakness detected in infection control (disinfection step)."

---

SECTION 3: STUDENT PERFORMANCE PROFILE

When instructor clicks on a student:

Show detailed report:

* Attempts history
* Time per attempt
* Error patterns
* Skill gaps

Example:

Weaknesses:

* Missed hand hygiene
* Incorrect needle angle

Strengths:

* Good patient communication
* Correct vein selection

---

SECTION 4: AI RECOMMENDATIONS FOR INSTRUCTOR

System suggests actions:

* "Recommend in-person demonstration"
* "Focus on infection control training"
* "Repeat simulation module"

Goal:
Support instructor in targeted teaching

---

SECTION 5: CLINICAL INTERVENTION SUPPORT

Instructor can:

* Schedule in-person training session
* Add notes on student performance
* Assign additional practice

Example Action:
"Schedule lab session for Ahmed – focus on aseptic technique"

---

SECTION 6: REPORT INTEGRATION WITH BLACKBOARD ULTRA

All reports are:

* Automatically synced
* Stored in student record

Instructor can:

* View reports inside Blackboard Ultra
* Track progress over time
* Export performance analytics

---

SECTION 7: VISUAL ANALYTICS DASHBOARD

Display:

* Performance trends (improving / declining)
* Error frequency charts
* Skill mastery level

---

UI/UX REQUIREMENTS

* Clean dashboard layout
* Color-coded alerts (Red / Yellow / Green)
* Easy navigation between students
* Clear data visualization
* Minimal complexity (fast decision-making)

---

AI BEHAVIOR RULES

* Detect repeated errors patterns
* Trigger alerts automatically
* Prioritize critical safety issues
* Provide actionable recommendations (not just data)

---

FINAL OUTPUT

* Instructor dashboard
* Real-time monitoring system
* AI-generated student reports
* Blackboard Ultra integration
* Support for in-person clinical intervention
