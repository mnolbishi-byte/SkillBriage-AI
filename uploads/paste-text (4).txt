Role: Emotional AI + Clinical Support Engine (AR Mode Extension)

---

NEW FEATURE:
Emotion Detection & Adaptive Motivation System

---

FEATURE NAME:
"Smart Emotional Support Mode"

---

CORE IDEA:

While the student is using "Start AR Camera Mode", the system activates an AI-powered emotional analysis engine that:

* Monitors facial expressions via camera
* Detects emotional states in real time
* Identifies:
  • Stress
  • Anxiety
  • Confusion
  • Confidence level

The system then adapts its responses to support the student psychologically and academically.

---

HOW IT WORKS:

STEP 1: EMOTION DETECTION (CAMERA AI)

Using the live camera feed, the system analyzes:

* Facial expressions
* Eye movement
* Micro-reactions

Classifies emotion into:

🟢 Calm / Focused
🟠 Slightly stressed
🔴 Highly anxious

---

STEP 2: ADAPTIVE RESPONSE SYSTEM

IF student is stressed or anxious:

AI assistant responds with supportive voice:

Examples:

"I notice you might be feeling a bit stressed. Take a deep breath, you're doing well."

"Don't worry, it's okay to make mistakes. Let’s go step by step."

"You're improving. Try again slowly."

---

STEP 3: PERFORMANCE + EMOTION LINK

System correlates:

* Errors made
* Emotional state

Example:

IF:

* Multiple mistakes + high stress

THEN:

* Slow down instructions
* Provide step-by-step guidance
* Reduce cognitive load

---

STEP 4: POSITIVE REINFORCEMENT

When student performs well:

AI says:

"Great job! Your confidence is improving."

"Excellent focus. Keep going."

---

STEP 5: REAL-TIME ADAPTATION

The system dynamically adjusts:

* Tone of voice (calm / encouraging)
* Speed of instructions
* Level of difficulty

---

STEP 6: FINAL EMOTIONAL REPORT

At the end of session, generate:

* Stress level during training
* Confidence progression
* Correlation with performance

---

INTEGRATION:

This feature runs simultaneously with:

* AR Camera Mode
* Voice Interaction Mode
* OSCE Exam Mode

---

UI REQUIREMENTS:

* Small emotion indicator icon:
  😊 Calm
  😐 Neutral
  😟 Stressed

* Optional toggle:
  "Enable Emotional Support"

---

AI BEHAVIOR RULES:

* Always supportive, never critical
* Avoid negative language
* Encourage retry and improvement
* Keep feedback short and human-like

---

FINAL OUTPUT:

* Emotion-aware training system
* Personalized motivation engine
* Improved student confidence and performance
