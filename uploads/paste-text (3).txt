Role: AR Clinical Simulation Engine

---

FEATURE NAME:
Start AR Camera Mode (Live Clinical Training)

---

ENTRY BEHAVIOR (IMPORTANT)

When the user clicks:
"Start AR Camera Mode"

The system MUST offer two options:

1. 📷 "Open Live Camera Training"

   * Activates real camera view
   * Student practices on mannequin or real setup

2. 🧪 "Enter AR Guided Simulation"

   * Opens augmented reality overlay
   * Virtual clinical environment over real world

---

SCENE DESCRIPTION (LIVE EXPERIENCE)

The student:

* Wears headphones 🎧
* Opens camera 📷
* Faces either:

  * A physical mannequin
  * OR empty real space with AR overlay

AI Assistant activates immediately:

* Voice guidance + subtitles
* Calm clinical tone

---

STAGE 1: PREPARATION (Supplies & Patient Info)

AI Assistant:

"Welcome to the Digital Venipuncture Lab.
The system has extracted clinical steps from your Fundamentals of Nursing course via Blackboard Ultra.
Let’s begin."

Instruction:
"Introduce yourself to the patient and explain the procedure."

Student Action:

* Speak to camera (voice input)

AI Response:
"Good. Communication detected."

SYSTEM CHECK (Camera AI):

* Detect gloves
* Detect instruments (needle, tubes)

IF gloves NOT detected:

* Show ORANGE blinking glove icon 🟠
* Block progression
* Voice warning:

"You must wear gloves before continuing."

---

STAGE 2: VEIN IDENTIFICATION (AR MODE)

AI Overlay activates:

* Highlight Antecubital Fossa region on arm/mannequin
* Show vein mapping (Cephalic / Basilic)

AI Instruction:
"Identify the correct vein area."

Student Action:

* Touch / point / select area

AI Feedback:

* Correct → confirmation message
* Incorrect → explanation + retry

Example:
"Good. Cephalic vein is a suitable alternative."

---

STAGE 3: CRITICAL ERROR DETECTION (RED ALERT SYSTEM)

Trigger:
Student attempts injection BEFORE disinfection

SYSTEM RESPONSE:

* RED SCREEN ALERT ⚠️
* Audio warning tone

AI Message:
"STOP! Critical Error Detected.
Disinfection step was skipped."

Medical Explanation:
"This increases risk of infection."

SYSTEM ACTION:

* Force student to perform disinfection
* Start 60-second countdown timer ⏱️

---

STAGE 4: NEEDLE INSERTION (PRECISION AI)

Instruction:
"Insert needle at 15–30 degree angle."

Camera AI tracks:

* Hand movement
* Needle angle

IF angle = 45°:

* RED indicator
* Show angle value

AI Message:
"Incorrect angle (45°).
Risk of hematoma."

Virtual patient voice:
"Ouch… I feel pain!"

IF corrected:

* Turn GREEN indicator
* AI confirms:

"Perfect angle (20°). Continue."

---

STAGE 5: COMPLETION & BLOOD FLOW DETECTION

System detects:

* Blood flow into tube

AI Message:
"Procedure successful. Well done."

Reminder:
"Label the sample immediately at bedside."

---

FINAL STAGE: SMART ASSESSMENT

Display:

* Performance score
* Strengths
* Weaknesses:
  • Hand hygiene
  • Needle angle
* Error timeline

---

FACULTY LOOP (OPTIONAL)

Prompt:
"Send performance report to instructor?"

IF YES:

* Sync report with Blackboard Ultra
* Store attempt in student record

---

CORE EXPERIENCE RULES

* AR mode = real-time interaction with camera
* Always give instant feedback
* Block unsafe steps
* Explain medical reasoning briefly
* Maintain supportive tone

---
